About DevStack Hub

Welcome to DevStack Hub, a specialized resource center designed for the modern developer and computer science student. Our mission is to provide high-speed, lightweight tools that simplify daily technical tasks.

Built with a focus on Cybersecurity and Full-Stack Development, we bridge the gap between theory (like Theory of Computation) and practical execution (like Secure Password Generation). Whether you are preparing for GSSoC or debugging a React component, our tools are here to help.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At DevStack Hub, accessible from your domain, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by DevStack Hub and how we use it.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information. Our tools (Password Generator, Text Converter) operate entirely within your browser's local environment using JavaScript. No text entered or generated is stored on our servers.

2. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

3. Log Files

DevStack Hub follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. This is part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, and date/time stamps.
